Problems: (3):

Inbreeding!

The colours of the rhinos indicate a family-bond. When 2 rhinos of the same family have offspring, their children have an increased chance of genetic diseases. So don't put males and females of the same colours in one enclosure.

Males fighting!

Male white rhinos get along perfectly fine, unless they are joined by a female. When that's the case, they'll fight over who gets the female. So make sure there's only one male with a group of females.

No male!

Without a male, these females can't get any offspring. We want them to, so don't leave any females without a male!

Enclosure overfull!

This enclosure doesn't have enough space for the amount of rhinos inside. There's only enough space for 5 rhinos!
