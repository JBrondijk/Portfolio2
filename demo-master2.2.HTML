DEMO: Augmented Reality for Zoo Education

Before you get started...

The following prototypes are designed to be used on mobile phones, but most of them require an image-target, which are displayed on this page. The designed context for these prototypes is the zoo, where the signs in this page would be put around the zoo. The tracking of these image targets works better when they are printed, so they might not respond fully when viewed on a screen like here.

It is recommended to open this page on a large screen (laptop/desktop computer), you can try pressing F11 to make the window full-screen. This should give the best result.
Scan the QR codes using your phone to start the demos, or use your phone to visit jurrienbrondijk.nl/demoday.
