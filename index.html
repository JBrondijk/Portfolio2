Who am I?

Iâ€™ve been practicing design ever since I started high school. There, I followed a Technasium track, where I did a number design projects similar to those I would later do studying industrial design. This was my main motivation for going starting this study, and to persue design going forward.

Over the course of my studies, I developed my skills as a designer, but more importantly, I found my passion for creating interactive educational experiences.
